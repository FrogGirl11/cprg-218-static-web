  • Who we are:

    In the search for ethical inclusive fashion, Leaf&Loom emerged. A sustainable clothing brand owned and run by women. Our vision is simple yet profound: to create ethical lounge wear that didn’t compromise on style or sustainability. Inspired by nature and driven by a commitment to do good, we carefully design each garment using eco-friendly materials like organic cotton and bamboo. But our mission reaches beyond just materials; it extends to fair wages, safe working conditions, and minimizing waste. Since it’s creation, Leaf&Loom has become more than just a clothing brand; it’s a movement. Women everywhere had embraced its message, joining a community of like-minded individuals who believe in fashion with a purpose. And so, Leaf&Loom’s story unfolds, culivating a culture of women empowering women, of style meeting sustainability. With every garment sold, they proved that fashion could be a force for good, changing the world one stitch at a time.
